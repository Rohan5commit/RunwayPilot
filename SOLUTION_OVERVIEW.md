# Solution Overview

RunwayPilot is designed as an AI CFO dashboard for non-finance operators. The product is fast to understand, practical in the real world, and intentionally focused on the smallest feature set that still creates serious business value.

## Product design

The experience starts with a simple choice: load demo data or upload a CSV. Within seconds, the app turns raw transactions into KPI cards, cashflow charts, spend diagnostics, runway forecasting, and an AI-generated operating summary. The interface is desktop-first, left-aligned, and organized so the user can understand what changed before exploring why it changed.

## Main workflows

1. Upload or load financial transactions.
2. Normalize and classify each transaction.
3. Review current burn, runway, and cash balance trend.
4. Inspect risky spend patterns such as anomalies, duplicated software, or vendor concentration.
5. Adjust a scenario like revenue down 20 percent or software down 15 percent.
6. Generate a founder-ready summary and recommended actions.

## Why this MVP is enough

The chosen feature set is deliberately narrow but complete. A team does not need a full ERP replacement to make better decisions. They need a faster feedback loop between cash activity and leadership action. The MVP delivers that loop through ingestion, classification, forecasting, risk detection, scenario planning, and reporting.

## How AI improves decisions without replacing judgment

RunwayPilot does not let AI invent the financial picture. Deterministic agents establish the facts first. The Strategy Agent then uses NVIDIA NIM to translate those facts into plain-language explanations, top risks, and action recommendations. This keeps the product credible: numbers come from logic, while AI improves clarity, prioritization, and communication.
