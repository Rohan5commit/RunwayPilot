# Project Overview

## Project title

RunwayPilot

## Problem summary

Early-stage teams and small businesses operate with fragmented financial visibility. Transactions live across bank exports, accounting tools, and spreadsheets, which means the team often notices runway compression only after spending patterns have already become expensive.

## Solution summary

RunwayPilot is an AI finance copilot that turns uploaded transaction data into runway forecasting, spend intelligence, anomaly detection, scenario simulation, and founder-ready recommendations. It combines deterministic finance logic with NVIDIA NIM explanations so users understand both the numbers and the actions behind them.

## What the product does

- ingests CSV or demo financial data
- classifies transactions into practical operating categories
- calculates burn, runway, monthly inflows, and cash balance trends
- flags anomalies, vendor concentration, and recurring waste
- simulates revenue and cost changes before they become real mistakes
- generates an exportable founder or investor update summary
